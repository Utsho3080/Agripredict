Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our smart crop prediction system

How accurate are the crop recommendations?

Our system maintains over 90% accuracy in crop recommendations based on extensive testing with real farm data across different regions and soil types.

What soil parameters do I need to input?

You need basic soil test results including NPK levels, pH value, electrical conductivity, organic carbon content, plus local climate data like temperature, humidity, and rainfall.

Can I use this for organic farming?

Yes! Our system includes organic farming recommendations and can suggest natural fertilizers and sustainable practices based on your soil conditions.

Is the service available in multiple languages?

Currently available in English, Hindi, and regional languages. We're continuously expanding language support based on user demand.
