About AgriTech

We're revolutionizing agriculture by combining cutting-edge AI technology with deep agricultural expertise to help farmers make data-driven decisions.

Our Story

Launched in early 2025, AgriTech is a major project developed by three dedicated students from Techno Main Salt Lake - Somodip Das, Utsho Saha, and Triasha Das, with a shared vision of transforming farming practices through technology.

What began as an ambitious academic project has evolved into a comprehensive platform designed to serve farmers across the country. Our team recognized that precise crop selection based on soil composition could significantly improve yields while reducing resource waste.

Over the past 4 months, we have worked intensively to develop prediction algorithms and create a user-friendly platform that bridges the gap between advanced agricultural science and practical farming solutions.
